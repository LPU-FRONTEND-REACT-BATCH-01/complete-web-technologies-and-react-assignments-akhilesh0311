Descriptive List of Biryani Preparation Steps

Clean the rice
Wash the rice thoroughly in cold water until the water runs clear.
Soak the rice
Soak the washed rice in water for 30 minutes.
Half boil rice
Boil the soaked rice until it is half-cooked.
Prepare Masala
Prepare the spice mix for the biryani.
Biryani is Prepared
Layer the half-cooked rice with the masala and cook until done.
