Understanding Semantic HTML5

Published on: October 24, 2023 by John Doe

What is Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML introduces meaning to the web page rather than just presentation. Tags like <article>, <section>, and <aside> clearly describe their context to both the browser and the developer.

Why is it Important?

It improves accessibility for screen readers and helps with SEO (Search Engine Optimization), allowing search engines to better understand the content of a page.
