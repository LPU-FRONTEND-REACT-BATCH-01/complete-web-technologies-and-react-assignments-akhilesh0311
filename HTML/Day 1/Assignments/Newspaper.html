Times of India

Times of India is an English-language daily newspaper in India. It is the third-largest newspaper in India by circulation and the largest selling English-language daily in the world.

History

Times of India was founded in 1838 as The Bombay Times and Journal of Commerce. It was renamed The Times of India in 1861. The newspaper has a long history of providing news and information to its readers, covering a wide range of topics including politics, business, sports, entertainment, and more.

Circulation

As of 2021, Times of India has a circulation of over 3 million copies daily. It is widely read across India and has a significant online presence as well.
